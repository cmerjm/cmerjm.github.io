MONOMANIE


As part of the training I made a drawing that I then reworked on Illustrator. I wanted to represent a heart that symbolizes life by using geometric shapes to transform this organic form into an abstract object. For that I assembled triangular shapes to have a geometrical appearance by using colors of cold tones to better understand the volume of these forms, we can distinguish the hat of the hen as a shell.

SHELL


As part of the training I made a drawing that I then reworked on Illustrator. I also left on geometric shapes to create the back of the shell followed by several curves to illustrate the body.
